PlayUEF

A free, web-based player for UEF-format Acorn cassette games. Just connect your 8-bit Acorn computer's cassette port to the headphone socket on a laptop or smartphone and you're ready to load games.

Source on GitHub

Load retro games from your browser

PlayUEF is a JavaScript player for UEF-format Acorn Electron and BBC Micro cassette games. Conversion from UEF to 44.1KHz WAV is done in the browser and played through a nice virtual cassette deck — no peripherals, no fuss. All you need is an Acorn retro machine and a compatible cassette lead.

Over 2,000 cassette games have been preserved in UEF, archived at Stairway to Hell. Pick your machine, find a title, and it loads straight into PlayUEF in your browser.
